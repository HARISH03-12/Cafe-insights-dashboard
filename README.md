### Business Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard
A dynamic and interactive "Business Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard" built in "Microsoft Excel"

## Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across different product categories, store locations, and products. It enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing products, compare store performance, and analyze revenue trends through interactive visualizations.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance.
- Monitor revenue, sales volume, and order count.
- Compare sales across multiple store locations.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Analyze revenue contribution by product category.
- Provide interactive filtering for deeper business insights.

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

The dashboard displays four key performance indicators:

- Total Revenue
- Sales Volume
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)

### Revenue, Sales Volume & Orders by Store

A combined visualization compares:

- Revenue generated by each store
- Product sales volume
- Total order count

### Top Selling Products

Displays the highest revenue-generating products.

Helps answer questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which products should receive additional marketing focus?
- Which products contribute most to overall revenue?

### Revenue by Category

Visualizes revenue generated across product categories such as:

- Coffee
- Tea
- Bakery
- Branded
- Coffee Beans
- Drinking Chocolate
- Packaged Chocolate
- Loose Tea
- Flavours

This enables quick identification of the most profitable product categories.

### Revenue & Sales Volume by Category

A comparative chart showing:

- Sales Volume
- Revenue

for each product category.

Useful for identifying:

- High-volume but low-revenue products
- High-revenue premium products
- Product categories requiring business attention.
